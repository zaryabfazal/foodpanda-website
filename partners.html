How does it work?

The customer adds their delivery address in the app or on the website and then selects a store or restaurant in their area.

You accept the order with the reading tablet and prepare it for the collection time.

A rider will come to you at the specified time and collect the order. It is then delivered directly to the customer!

Twice per month you receive payments for the orders you have received via foodora. You automatically receive a basis for the deposit and a detailed insight into your sales statistics.

How can I receive orders?
There are two ways to receive orders. Via a tablet, or if you have some kind of restaurant software (POS provider), it is also possible to connect it. In this case, indicate this during the connection process.
Are there any costs if I register on the site?
There is no cost for registering on the site. foodpanda only charges a commission if a sale has taken place. So you only have to pay when you sell something.
